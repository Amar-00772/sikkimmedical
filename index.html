About Sikkim Medical Council

Established in 2007 under an Act of Parliament, Sikkim Medical Council is a premier central university nestled in the serene Himalayan landscape of Gangtok. We are committed to excellence in teaching, research, and community engagement.

With state-of-the-art infrastructure, world-class faculty, and interdisciplinary programs, we nurture future leaders in arts, science, technology, management, and social sciences.

Our campus promotes sustainability, cultural diversity, and innovation while preserving the rich heritage of Sikkim.
